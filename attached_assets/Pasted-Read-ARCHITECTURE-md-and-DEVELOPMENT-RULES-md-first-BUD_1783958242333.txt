   - priorAttorney === true → "Disqualified", reason: "Already has counsel"

     check is fine — e.g. "broken", "surgery", "hospital", "concussion" vs.
     minor/no mention) → "Hot"; anything else that passed the above checks
     → "Warm"
   Keep the keyword logic simple and commented — this is a first-pass
   heuristic, not final, and should be easy for a human to read and adjust
   later without needing to understand the whole file.
