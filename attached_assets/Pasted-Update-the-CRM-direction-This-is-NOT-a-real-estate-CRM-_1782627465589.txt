CORE CRM PURPOSE

The CRM should help SiteMint Digital track a lead from first inquiry to discovery questionnaire, proposal, signed project, active build, launch, maintenance, and future upsell.

Primary use cases:

A business owner submits a website or app inquiry.
The CRM creates/updates the lead.
The lead is classified by project type.
The lead is routed into the correct nurture or sales campaign.
The system can generate a scope of work and proposal from the discovery questionnaire.
The team can manage the project through design, development, review, launch, and support.
The CRM tracks all emails, SMS, notes, tasks, campaign activity, and client history.

IMPORTANT RULES

Use Drizzle migrations for all database changes.
Do not use automatic schema sync in production.
Use dotenv environment variables.
Webhook endpoints must bypass API-key auth.
Public webhook route matching must use req.originalUrl, not req.path.
Campaign step saves should replace the full step list to preserve ordering.
Scheduled campaign cadence must advance based on the actual send time.
Email/SMS sends must respect send windows.
Messages due outside business hours should reschedule to the next available window.
SMS/email providers must use a .send() interface and should not be hardcoded throughout the app.
