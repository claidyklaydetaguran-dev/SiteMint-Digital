TASK 3 — ENGAGEMENT SCORE LOGIC
===============================

Use transparent rule-based scoring.

Example scoring:

Base score: 30

Positive:
+20 recent inbound reply within 7 days
+15 two-way conversation exists
+10 outbound message sent within 3 days
+10 completed call
+10 task/follow-up scheduled
+5 email sent
+5 SMS sent

Negative:
-15 no response after 3 outbound attempts
-20 no inbound reply ever
-20 no communication in 14 days
-25 no communication in 30 days
-10 failed SMS/call status

Clamp between 0 and 100.

Badge:
90–100 Highly Engaged
70–89 Engaged
50–69 Moderate
30–49 Low Engagement
0–29 Cold

Every score must include reasons.
