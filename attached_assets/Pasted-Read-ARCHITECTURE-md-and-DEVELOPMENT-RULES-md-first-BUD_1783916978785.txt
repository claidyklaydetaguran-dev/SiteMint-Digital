Context: this is Phase 1 of a new product — an AI SMS intake agent for law
firms. Full spec is below. This phase builds the core mechanism only: no
scoring, no notifications, no UI. Just prove the conversation loop works:
inbound SMS → LLM extracts structured facts across multiple exchanges →
reply sent back → data persisted. Testable entirely via curl simulating
Twilio's webhook payload format — no real Twilio number needs to be
provisioned yet.

--- SPEC SUMMARY ---
A law firm's intake line receives a text. An AI conversationally interviews
the sender to extract: incidentType, incidentDate, injurySeverity,
faultDescription, priorAttorney (bool), and confirms contact info. It
replies naturally over multiple SMS exchanges (this is not a rigid form —
use the LLM to hold a natural conversation while extracting structured data
as a side effect of that conversation). Once enough fields are known or the
sender indicates they're done, the conversation is marked complete. Scoring
and notification are Phase 2, not this task.
--- END SPEC SUMMARY ---
