Build a modern, premium website for a website/web app development business.

Business goal:
Create a clear, high-converting website that sells website creation, web application development, SEO support, blog/content support, and ongoing business growth services.

Brand style:
Modern, clean, professional, Apple-style layout.
Use neutral luxury colors: white, soft gray, black, beige, muted gold or soft blue accent.
Tone should feel trustworthy, smart, helpful, and business-focused — not too techy.

Website pages:
1. Home
2. Services
3. Pricing
4. Portfolio / Case Studies
5. About
6. Contact / Book a Call

Homepage sections:
- Hero section with headline:
  “Websites and Web Apps Built to Help Your Business Grow”
- Subheadline:
  “We create clean, professional websites, custom web applications, SEO foundations, blog systems, and automation tools that help businesses look credible and operate smarter.”
- CTA buttons:
  “Book a Free Consultation”
  “View Services”
- Trust/value section:
  Fast turnaround, mobile-friendly design, SEO-ready structure, business automation, ongoing support
- Services preview
- Pricing preview
- Process section:
  Discover → Design → Build → Launch → Optimize
- FAQ
- Final CTA

Design requirements:
- Use cards for pricing tiers
- Use icons for services
- Make pricing easy to compare
- Add “Starting at” pricing placeholders instead of fixed prices
- Add “Book a Consultation” buttons on every pricing card
- Include FAQ section explaining the difference between a website and a web app
- Include a contact form with name, email, phone, business type, service needed, budget range, and message

Important copy direction:
Make the business sound like it helps owners not just “get a website,” but build a digital system that attracts leads, builds trust, and supports daily operations.

Add strong CTA language:
- “Let’s build your business online”
- “Start with a website. Grow with systems.”
- “Turn your website into a business tool.”

Make the website fully responsive for desktop and mobile.
Use clean reusable components.
Use modern animations subtly, not too flashy.
Include placeholder portfolio projects and testimonials.
