2. INBOX → real conversations, not fake tickets

Replace every call to /api/helpdesk/tickets and /api/helpdesk/messages in

GET /api/receptionist/conversations and

leaving them wired to nothing. Show the real TierBadge (Hot/Warm/Cold/
Disqualified/Needs Review) using the tier field already in the API
response.

The message composer has no real backend today — the AI Receptionist
product has never had a "business owner manually replies" feature, only
automated AI responses the owner reviews. Do NOT wire the Send button to
anything fake. Leave the composer visually present but disabled with a
"Manual replies coming soon" note, OR hide it entirely — your call on
which reads better, but it must not appear to send a message that goes
nowhere. This is a real product gap worth flagging back, not something to
paper over.
