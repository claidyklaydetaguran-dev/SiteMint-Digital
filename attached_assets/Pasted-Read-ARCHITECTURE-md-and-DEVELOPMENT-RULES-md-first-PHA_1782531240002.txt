PHASE 15 — Sales Intelligence Hub (Next Best Action + Opportunity Radar + Daily Sales Brief)

MODULE:
CRM Dashboard + Sales Workspace + Lead Detail

IMPORTANT:
This phase is NOT AI.

Do NOT use OpenAI or any LLM.

Everything must be transparent, deterministic, explainable, and rule-based.

Reuse the existing engines whenever possible:

* leadScore.ts
* communicationIntelligence.ts
* workflowEngine.ts
* discEngine.ts

Do not modify these engines unless absolutely required.

Do not touch:

* Twilio
* Campaign sending
* Resend
* Locked modules
* Database schema unless a very small addition becomes absolutely necessary.

Stay within the normal change budget.
