PHASE 16 — Sales Automation Engine (Human-in-the-Loop)

MODULE:
Discovery Portal
CRM
Sales Workspace

IMPORTANT:

This phase is NOT AI.

Do NOT use OpenAI.

Everything must be deterministic.

Nothing should automatically contact customers.

Nothing should send emails or SMS automatically.

Everything should generate drafts and recommendations for human approval.

Do not modify locked modules.

Do not touch Twilio.

Do not modify Campaign sending.

Reuse existing engines.

Stay within the default change budget.

---

OBJECTIVE

Create a centralized Sales Automation Engine that prepares work for the salesperson automatically whenever a lead enters or changes state.

The CRM should become proactive rather than reactive.

---
