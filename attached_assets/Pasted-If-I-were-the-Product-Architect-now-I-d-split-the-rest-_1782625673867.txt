My biggest recommendation

Stop thinking in terms of phases.

Start thinking in terms of products inside your CRM.

If your goal is for SiteMint CRM to compete with platforms like HighLevel, HubSpot, Follow Up Boss, Salesforce, or Zoho, this shift in strategy is what will get you there much faster.

One recommendation before you start this phase

I would stop asking Replit to implement everything in one shot. Instead, ask it to build one complete system like the prompt above. A system can contain 20–40 related features, but they all serve one cohesive purpose. This reduces the number of prompts you need while making it much easier to test and review the result. It also keeps the architecture cleaner than dozens of tiny phases.
