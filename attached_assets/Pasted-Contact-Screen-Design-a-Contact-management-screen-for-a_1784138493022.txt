Contact Screen
"Design a Contact management screen for a helpdesk CRM. Show a dense table with columns: Name, Last Contact, Note, Action. Include a top-right toolbar with 'MCP Access,' 'Export,' and a primary 'Add Contact' button. When no contacts exist, show a centered empty state with a light gray line-art illustration, a bold 'No data' headline, and a muted helper sentence below. Apply the shared design system: white/light-gray background, 1px hairline dividers, navy primary buttons, and high information density."
Agent / Settings Screen
"Design a nested Settings screen with a secondary sidebar grouped into four sections: Efficiency (Agent Configuration), People (Members), Account (MCP Access, Language Settings), and Resources (Product Guide, Download Mobile App). The Agent Configuration panel shows an empty state with a centered icon, a bold 'Select an Agent' headline, and muted helper text. The Members panel shows a 'Member Management' table with columns Email, Name, Online Status (green dot pill), Creation Date, and Last Active Time, plus a search bar and an 'Add Member' button top-right. Use skeleton-pulse loading placeholders shaped like table rows, and the shared navy/indigo/muted-gray palette throughout."
Deploy / Integrations Screen
"Design a Deploy screen with a top-level card grid of three channel cards: Email ('Receive and reply to customer tickets via email,' purple envelope badge), Phone ('Manage Twilio phone numbers, take calls and send SMS,' green phone badge), and Live Chat ('Embed real-time chat on your site to talk with customers,' blue chat-bubble badge). Clicking a card drills into its own management screen: Email management lists connected inboxes in a table (Email, Brand, Status, Authorization Time) with a '+ Email' button; Phone management lists numbers in a table (Phone Number, Calls, SMS ON/OFF toggle, Source, Actions menu) with a '+ Phone Number' button; Live Chat shows an empty state ('Connect your website's LiveChat widget') with a '+ Livechat' button. Use rounded, soft-shadow cards for the grid and dense hairline-divided tables for the drill-down screens."
Billing Screen
"Design a Billing screen with Plan and Usage tabs. Plan tab: trial status with countdown/end date, an upgrade banner, a Plan Details card (seats, extra credit pack, team number), a Current Access summary card (channel access, trial phone number, credits available, AI assistance note), and a sticky right-side checkout card with a seat-quantity stepper, credit-pack dropdown, order summary (plan, seats, extra credits, pay-today total, renewal note), and a 'Continue to payment' button. Usage tab: a date-range picker, a large 'Credits used this period' figure, a four-stat row (Granted, Used, Reserved, Available), a Credit Activity log of grant events, and a Breakdown-by-category panel. Use navy CTAs, indigo/purple trial accents, and compact stat cards."
